Test Assignment

The task is to calculate the difference between two given dates. It covers leap years in between them as well.

  • step 1 : It validates the textboxes for values .
  • step 2 : If it's a matched value with date regex it goes on.
  • step 3 : Split the date and break it into days, months and year.
  • step 4 : Both months and years are converted into days from the very begining of time(00:00:0000)
  • step 5 : Next add all the day's, months and years to get everything in days.
  • step 6 : The above steps are executed for both dates
  • step 6 : The result is a integer for both with the day
  • step 7 : Both day's will be subtract to get the difference.
